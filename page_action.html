c:[012345]+
Search for cards with a certain number of colors.
  • c:23rg
    Search for 2- or 3-color cards that are Red and/or Green.
  • c!3rg
    Search for 3-color cards that are both Red and Green.
ci![wubrg]+
Search for cards with an exact color identity.
  • ci!rg
    Search for cards whose color identity is exactly Red and Green.
  • ci!rg -c:m
    Search only for colorless/mono-color cards with Red or Green mana symbols in their textboxes.
cw[:!][wubrg]+
Search for cards that are castable with specific colors.
  • cw:rg
    Search for Red/Green cards that are castable using just Red and Green mana.
  • cw!r
    Search for non-mono-Red cards that are castable using just Red mana.
